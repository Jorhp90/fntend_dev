Gengar!

Gengar (ゲンガー, Gengaa) is a Ghost/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I.

Physiology: Gengar is a shadow-like, round-bodied Pokémon with two large, pointed ears and short spikes lining its back. Gengar has two red eyes and a toothy, sinister smile. It has claws on its hands. Gengar stands on its two short legs, but it can Levitate as well.
